About Hypercard

Hypercard! is a simple visual novel in which you interact with a super-advanced digital assistant, what could possibly go wrong?

Features!

Short gameplay.

A gameplay of only half an hour in the full game, but full of small details.

Fully voice acted.

The game is fully acted with lines that can change as you progress, they can change too much.

Open choices.

In certain sections of the game there are open-ended decisions in which you can answer whatever you want, and these will be reflected in the future.

Multiple endings.

Within the game, based on your decisions, there can be different endings, each in unique circumstances, great, isn't it?
